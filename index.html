Eight (8) double niche memorial lots are designed for the placement of cremated remains. Each double niche accommodates two urns within a shared memorial space, allowing companions or family members to be interred together. The niches are typically enclosed with a secure, front-facing panel suitable for personalized inscriptions, nameplates, or memorial plaques. Lawn Lot

A lawn lot memorial is a ground burial space situated within a landscaped lawn setting, offering a serene and dignified place of remembrance. The lot accommodates the interment of a casket and is typically marked with a flat or upright memorial marker in accordance with cemetery guidelines. Lawn lots provide a traditional burial option within a well-maintained, park-like environment designed for lasting tribute and visitation.

Modern Style Mausoleum

A modern style mausoleum is an above-ground interment structure featuring clean lines, contemporary design, and durable materials such as granite or marble. It provides secure entombment with personalized crypt or niche panels, offering a dignified and lasting place of remembrance.

Manila Memorial Park - Sta. Rosa

Manila Memorial Park in Sta. Rosa offers beautifully landscaped memorial grounds with a peaceful ambiance. Featuring pristine lawns, elegant architectural elements, and serene water features, this location provides families with a dignified and tranquil final resting place in a modern memorial park setting.

Manila Memorial Park - Sucat

Located in Sucat, Manila Memorial Park is one of the most prestigious memorial parks in the Philippines. Offering memorial lots, columbary niches, and cremation services, this beautifully maintained facility features the Rizal Premier Chapel and provides families with a respectful, accessible location for remembrance and memorial services.

Memorial Lot Structure

A memorial lot is a designated burial space within a cemetery or memorial park. It provides a permanent location for interment, typically marked with a headstone or plaque, offering a respectful and lasting place for remembrance and visitation. Our lots feature quality construction with vault protection and customizable memorial markers.

Chapel Lobby

The Chapel Lobby is a welcoming gathering space located at the entrance of the memorial chapel. It serves as a reception and waiting area for family and guests before and after services, offering a comfortable setting for quiet reflection, condolences, and fellowship in a respectful memorial environment.

Memorial Urns

Urns are containers designed to hold cremated remains, offering a dignified way to preserve and honor a loved one. Available in a variety of materials, styles, and finishes, they can be personalized with inscriptions or decorative elements and may be displayed in a columbarium, mausoleum, or kept at home as a lasting memorial.
